I love hikning more then most things in life. I have been on such amazing hikes and have been able to see some of the most beutiful things that God has created. I know that hiking helps me feel better and I love the things I get to see and how I feel after a great hike.

Benifits of being outdoors

Trying New Things

Being outside can help people experience new things which can help them find more joy in life.

Less Technology

Being outdoors helps reduce the amount of time on technology and increases the amount of time spent outside.

Feel Gods Love

Being outdoors can help people feel and see the love that God has for them as He has given us such a beautiful planet.
